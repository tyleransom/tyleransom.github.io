About me

When I am not working, I am raising my children, volunteering at my church and in the community as a Boy Scouts of America troop scoutmaster, cooking, reading books, listening to podcasts, tweeting, or (occasionally) blogging. I am a LaTeX enthusiast and enjoy following sports and doing sports analytics. My favorite teams are: BYU, Chelsea FC, Duke, and the Utah Jazz.
